FAQs.

Everything you need to know about the platform and our methodology.

Can I watch later?

Yes! Every live class is recorded and uploaded to your student dashboard within 24 hours. You can watch replays anytime if you miss a session.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes. Upon successful completion of your projects and final review, you will receive a verified certificate. You can view sample certificates in our prospectus.

How much time do I need?

Most programs require 6 to 8 hours per week. This covers live training sessions and independent practice. See full schedules on our curriculum page.

What if I don't like it?

We want you to love your experience. If you are not satisfied within the first 7 days, we offer a full refund. Review our terms here.

Do I need a fancy computer?

A standard laptop and stable internet are sufficient for 90% of our classes. Specific tech requirements are listed on each course page.

Will I have friends there?

Yes! Community is at our core. You'll join a cohort of peers and get direct access to our alumni network for lifelong connections.

Do I need to know stuff already?

We have paths for everyone! From "101" masterclasses for beginners to advanced tracks for pros. Check the level on our all-courses grid.

Are there group discounts?

Yes! We provide custom packages for teams and organizations. Please visit our For Teams page or contact us.

How do I pay?

We accept all major cards and bank transfers via our secure portal. Payment plans are also available for select tracks.

Can I get a job after?

Absolutely. We provide career coaching, portfolio reviews, and direct introductions to our hiring partners once you finish.

Who are the Trainers?

Our trainers and mentors are active professionals at companies like Google and Microsoft. They teach real-world skills. Meet them here.

Is it only for young people?

Not at all. We have students ranging from age 15 to 65. Our trainers are patient and use easy-to-understand language. Read our story.

What if I miss a class?

Don't worry! Every session is recorded. You can catch up by watching the video in your portal as soon as you're free.

Do I talk to my Trainer?

Yes! All our classes are live. You can ask questions, share your screen, and get feedback from your trainer in real-time. Sign up for a demo.

Is there an early discount?

Yes! We offer "Early Bird" discounts for most cohorts. Check the individual class pages on our portal for current offers.

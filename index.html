FAQs.

Everything you need to know about the platform and our methodology.

Are the sessions recorded?

Yes, all live sessions are recorded and made available in your dashboard within 2 hours, though live attendance is highly recommended for the best experience.

Do I get a certificate?

Upon successful completion of projects and the final capstone, you will receive a verified industry-standard certificate shareable on LinkedIn.

What is the weekly commitment?

Typically, students spend 6-8 hours a week: 4 hours of live instruction and 2-4 hours on hands-on project work.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 100% money-back guarantee within the first week of the cohort if you feel the course isn't the right fit for you.

Do I need a high-end laptop?

Most courses only require a stable internet connection and a mid-range laptop (i5 processor, 8GB RAM). Specific tech tracks like Engineering will have detailed requirements sent after enrollment.

Is there a community for alumni?

Absolutely. Once you graduate, you gain lifetime access to our exclusive Slack community, where we share global job openings and hold monthly networking events.

What are the prerequisites?

Our tracks range from Beginner to Advanced. Each course page details specific skills needed, but most of our "101" masterclasses require zero prior experience.

Are there group discounts?

Yes! For teams of 5 or more, we offer custom pricing. Please visit our "For Teams" page or contact our support team for a tailored quote.

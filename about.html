The Problem.

The "Zombie" Learning Crisis

Most platforms struggle with engagement because learning alone is difficult. Pre-recorded videos often lack mentorship, accountability, practical feedback, and genuine human connection. Many aspiring professionals in Africa also face pricing barriers and learning systems disconnected from local realities..

The Sabicrest Solution

We solved this by making learning live, collaborative, and mentorship-driven. No isolated learning experiences here. We built a professional ecosystem where people can learn directly from vetted experts, build alongside peers, access localized opportunities, and develop industry-ready skills through active participation.

The Problem.

The "Zombie" Learning Crisis

Most platforms boast 5% completion rates. Why? Because pre-recorded videos are lonely, outdated, and lack accountability. Students in Nigeria often face FX barriers and curricula that don't reflect the local market realities.

The Sabicrest Solution

We solved this by making learning 100% Live. No "Zombies" here. We localized pricing to Naira to remove FX volatility and built a direct pipeline from our classrooms to top-tier global internships.
